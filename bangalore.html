Bangalore Tour

Meet the Guest on arrival and transfer to hotel for rest and relaxation. Based on the operational possibilities and Guest’s arrival time, Vehicle will be provided for the Guest’s own choice of programmes i.e. sightseeing, shopping, entertainment, village tour, meeting friends and relatives and cultural programmes like dance, music, cinema, etc. at the arrival city. Vehicle is placed at the Guest’s disposal. Overnight Stay Chennai.

Sightseeing Marina Beach, Kap aleeswar temp le, Fort St. George, Museum, Santhome Church, Apparswamy temple and St. Thomas Mount. Overnight Stay Chennai.

Drive Chennai to Bangalore 6 Hrs. Sightseeing- Lalbagh, Vidhana Soudha, Mahalakshmi temple, Garuda Vahana Anjaneya temple, Infant Jesus Church, Iskcon Krishna temple and Cubbon Park. Overnight Stay Bangalore.

Drive Bangalore to Mysore 3 Hrs. via Bangalore Film City. Sightseeing Chamundeswari temple, Philomena Church, Brindavan Garden, Tipu Sultan’s Summer Palace and Maharaja city Palace. Overnight Stay Mysore.

Drive Mysore to Ooty 4 Hrs. Sightseeing Botanical Garden, Lake, Doddabetta and Childrens’ Park. Overnight Stay Ooty.

Drive Ooty to Guruvayur 4 Hrs. Sightseeing Lord Krishna Temple, Vadakunathan Shiva temple, Palayur Oldest Church, Punnathur Kotta Elephant Camp and Mammiyoor Shiva temple. Overnight Stay Guruvayur.

Drive Ooty to Guruvayur 4 Hrs. Sightseeing Lord Krishna Temple, Vadakunathan Shiva temple, Palayur Oldest Church, Punnathur Kotta Elephant Camp and Mammiyoor Shiva temple. Overnight Stay Guruvayur.

Drive Cochin to Alleppey 2 Hrs for Boating to explore Back Water Resources and sightseeing. Overnight Stay Alleppey.

Drive Alleppey to Trivandrum 3 Hrs. Sightseeing Haripad Mannarsala Snake temple, Sri Padmanabhaswamy temple, Palace Museum.

Drive – Trivandrum to Kovalam – ½ Hr. Sightseeing Parasurama temple, Beaches, Koyikkal Palace Numismatic Museum and Folklore Museum, Vellayani Lake and Kaudiar Palace. Overnight Stay Kovalam.

Drive Kovalam to Kanyakumari 2 Hrs. Sightseeing Padmanabha Palace, Kumari Amman Temple, Vivekananda Rock, Beach, SunSet, SunRise, Moon Rise and Suchindram Sthanumanalyan Temple. Over night stay in Kanyakumarai

Drive Kanyakumari to Madurai 6 Hrs. Sightseeing Meenakshi Amman Temple, Thirumalai Nayakkar Mahal, Pazhamudir Solai Murugan temple, Thiruparamkundram Murugan Temple, Rakhaee Amman temple and Karuppannaswamy temple. Overnight Stay Madurai.

Drive Madurai to Trichy 2 Hrs. Sightseeing Srirangam Ranganatha Swamy temple, Rockfort Uchipillayar temple and Samayapuram Mariamman temple. Overnight Stay Trichy.

Drive Trichy to Tanjore 1 Hr for visiting Big temple.

Drive Tanjore to Vaitheeswaran Koil 2 Hrs Vaitheeswaran Koil Navagraha Sevvai temple.

Drive Vaitheeswaran Koil to Chidambaram-1 Hr for visiting Nataraja and Thillai Kali temple.

Drive Chidambaram to Pondicherry 1 Hr. Sightseeing Auroville Aurobindo Ashram, Manakula Vinayagar temple and Beach.Drive – Pondicherry to Mahabalipuram 1½ Hrs. Overnight Stay Mahabalipuram

Sightseeing Five Rathas, Arjuna’s Penance, Krishna Mandapam, Shore temple and Beach.

Drive Mahabalipuram to Kancheepuram 2 Hrs. Sightseeing Vardharaja Perumal temple, Ekambareswarar temple, Kamakshi Amman temple and Ulaganatha Perumal temple.

Drive Kancheepuram to Chennai 2 Hrs. Overnight Stay Chennai.
