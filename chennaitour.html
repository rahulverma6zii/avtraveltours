Chennai Tour

Meet the Guest on arrival and transfer to hotel for rest and relaxation. Sightseeing – Marina Beach, Kapaleeswar temple, Fort St. George, Museum, Santhome Church, Apparswamy temple and St.Thomas Mount. Overnight Stay Chennai.

Drive Chennai to Thirupathi – 4 Hrs. Sightseeing – Lord Venkateswara temple and on the way visit Tiruttani Murugan temple, Masur Goddess Mahishasura Mardhini temple and Thiruchanoor Goddess Padmavathi temple. Overnight Stay – Thirupathi

Drive Thirupathi to Kancheepuram 3 Hrs. Sightseeing Varadharaja Perumal temple, Ekambareswarar temple, Kamakshi Amman temple and Ulaganatha Perumal temple.Drive Kancheepuram to Thiruvannamalai 1 Hr for visiting Arunachaleswarar temple and Sithargal Ashrams. Overnight Stay – Thiruvannamalai.

Drive Thiruvannamalai to Pondicherry 4 Hrs. Sightseeing Auroville Aurobindo Ashram, Manakula Vinayagar temple and Beach.Drive Pondicherry to Mahabalipuram 2 Hrs. Sightseeing Five Rathas, Arjuna’s Penance, Krishna Mandapam, Shore Temple and Beach. Drive Mahabalipuram to Chennai 1½ Hrs. Overnight Stay in Chennai.

“ Transfer ” to Airport or Railway Station for return journey after completion of tour. Based on the Guest’s Departure time and operational possibilities, Vehicle will be provided for Guest’s own choice of programmes i.e. sightseeing, shopping, entertainment, village tour, meeting friends & relatives and cultural programmes like dance, music, cinema, etc at Departure city. Vehicle is placed at the Guest’s disposal
